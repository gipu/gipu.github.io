Collegesquare

In the heart of the city of Calcutta( now spelt as Kolkata ) there is College Street with College Square on it. A seat of learning and intellectual activities since the days of the nineteenth century, it houses some of the leading educational institutions of the city, even of the country. There are the University of Calcutta (College Street Campus), Presidency College (1817) , Sanskrit College, Hindu School(1817), Hare School (1819), Sanskrit Collegiate School (1824) and the Calcutta Medical College and Hospital; there are the historic institutes and buildings of the University Institute, the Baptist Mission Students’ Hall and the Mahabodhi Society. The ambience must have fuelled our Alma Mater, the Presidency College, to live upto the height it reached.We span around the College Square to set the settings right.
