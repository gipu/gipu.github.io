A K DEY: b.1901,d.     : alumnus: B Sc1923: Joined GSI 1923; Superintending Geologist 1947: Professor 1945-47: Made detailed studies of Eastern and Northeastern India : Coauthor, with DUNN, of the famous Memoir on Bihar and Orissa: Member, Planning Commission.

A B DUTT: b.1904: alumnus: M Sc1926:Joined GSI 1929: Lecturer 1948-49: Studied many parts of Burma, coalfields of Bengal & Bihar; on reserves of coking and non-coking coals; Member,Coal Survey Commission; Fuel Research Institute.

A K GHOSH: b.1905, d.n.a.: alumnus: B Sc 1926: M Sc Botany CU 1928:Joined GSI for a brief period: Professor of botany in Diocesan College, Victoria Institution, South Calcutta Girls’ College: Lecturer Botany CU1937-48:  Also Honorary Lecturer in Geology Presidency College : 1948 onwards Registrar Bose Research Institute Calcutta: Important contributions in palynology of coal and other sedimentary deposits

J B AUDEN: b.1903, d.n.a.: Joined GSI 1926; Superintending Geologist 1945: Professor 1940-42:Important studies on different parts of the Himalayas; Minerals of Bihar, Pb-Zn of Zawar; geological problems associated with landslides, hydel projects, irrigation schemes, water supply etc.; assd. with scientific institutions:

L A N IYER: b.1892,d. n.a.: Joined GSI 1922: Lecturer 1943-45; Tata Prof. in Patna Univ. 1945:Author “Handbook of Precious Stones”:ISC (Pr., Geol.) 1952.

D R S MEHTA: b.1917,d.n.a.: Joined GSI 1940: Lecturer 1949-51:Made surveys on glass-making sands, ceramic raw materials.
